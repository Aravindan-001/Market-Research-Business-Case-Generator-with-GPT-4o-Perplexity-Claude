This system automatically conducts market research and generates complete business cases by combining the strengths of GPT-4o, Perplexity, and Claude.

What it does:

Collects and analyzes market information from multiple AI sources

Generates clear, structured market research reports

Converts research insights into professional business cases

Produces decision-ready documentation with minimal manual effort

How it works (conceptually):

Takes an input such as a business idea, product concept, industry, or problem statement

Uses Perplexity to gather up-to-date market insights, trends, competitors, and data points

Uses GPT-4o to synthesize information, structure analysis, and build logical narratives

Uses Claude for deeper reasoning, clarity, and risk-focused evaluation
